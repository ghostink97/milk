A fact a day keeps the doctor away

DAY #1 GET YOUR FIRST BOX OF PLANT-BASED MILK

The best way to start your 12 day cow-milk free experience is to go to the store and find yourself a 1l of plant based milk. What are your options? Most popular vegan milks are made of soy, oats, almond and coconut and they would be our recommendation for your first plant-based milk experience. Don’t forget to try it after you come back from the store!
